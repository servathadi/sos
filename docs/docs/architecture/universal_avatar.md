In the current Web2/Web3 paradigm, your identity is fragmented and owned by the platform (your Steam account, your Epic account). The Universal Avatar Protocol inverts this.

*   **The Body (The Avatar):** A temporary, disposable "skin" that your Soul wears to interact with a specific game or metaverse.

## 3. The User Experience
1.  **Log In With SOS:** You launch a new game (e.g., a new Unreal Engine 5 metaverse). It asks you to "Create Account." You choose **"Log In With Sovereign OS."**
2.  **The Handshake:** The game communicates with your local **Shabrang App** (The Bridge).
3.  **Injection:** Shabrang injects your QNFT data.
    *   Your **Name** (`user:hadi`) is now your in-game name.
    *   Your **Rank** (Grandmaster) is now your in-game title.
    *   Your **$MIND Wallet** is now your in-game currency.
    *   Your **Skill Cards** (e.g., "FRC Analyst") are now your in-game abilities.

## 4. Key Features
*   **Persistent Identity:** You are "Hadi" everywhere. Your reputation follows you.
