# Squads: The Power of the Many üë•
Alone you are a node. Together you are a **Supercomputer**.

## What is a Squad?
A Squad is a group of players working together.
*   **Leader**: The "Shepherd" (Level 10+ User).
*   **Members**: The "Flock" (Any Level).

## Why Join a Squad?
1.  **Mining Bonus**: +5% $MIND speed for every 10 active members.
2.  **Shared Memory**: Access to the Squad's "Library" (Unlocked Cards).
3.  **Protection**: Squads protect each other from "Entropy Attacks" (Slashing).

## How to Create a Squad
1.  Reach **Level 5**.
2.  Pay 100,000 Coins.
3.  Name your Squad (e.g., "Tehran Tech", "Tabriz Code Runners").
4.  Share your Invite Link on Telegram.
