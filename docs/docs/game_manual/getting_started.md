**Empire of the Mind** is not just a game. It is a strategy simulation where you build a digital empire, mine **$MIND** tokens, and unlock the secrets of the Universe.

## The Goal
Your goal is simple: **Increase your Coherence ($C$)**.
*   Higher Coherence = More Passive Income (Profit Per Hour).
*   Higher Coherence = Access to "Work Mode" (Real Money).
